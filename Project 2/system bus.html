System Buses

A system bus is a single computer bus that connects the major components of a computer system, combining the functions of a data bus to carry information, an address bus to determine where it should be sent, and a control bus to determine its operation. The components of computer are interacted by the bus. There are different types of bus structure. The bus is a medium used to transfer data, memory address and control signal from one part to other part of computers. There are three types of bus:
  1. Data Bus
  2. Control Bus
  3. Control Bus
